What is Bonus?

Bonus questions are questions released by the professor during the week, based on the topics taught during the week, to be used for practice. They are released 3-4 days before lab-day and are extremely helpful in understanding the material. Doing the bonus questions is a sure-fire way to score good in labs.

Statistically speaking, some are very daring they skip practice, some are very genius they verify other's solution. Well, I thank them for lowering average rather to give other oppurtunity to score A/B grade.
