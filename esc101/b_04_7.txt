You've just learned about capital and small case in your language class. Fascinated by their beauty, you decide to write a program to convert them into the other i.e. transform a small-case into capital-case and vice versa. You are given a character (Either small or caps), you need to print the corresponding character in the opposite case.

Input: Input contains a character (Either small-case or capital-case).
Output: The corresponding letter in the opposite case i.e. small case if original was capital-case; capital case if original was small-case.

Example: 

Input:
R
Output:
r
Input:
c
Output:
C

Note: Do not hard-code the solutions i.e. explicitly write the answer for each possible input (Doing so would lead to deduction of up to 80% marks). Arrive at a clean and elegant solution.</div>
