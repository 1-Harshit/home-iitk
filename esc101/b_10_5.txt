                <div style="white-space: pre-wrap" class="statement">Karan has a plot of land he wants to sell. He has informed several brokers to find buyers for the land. Each broker has given Karan a list of buyers sorted in descending order of the price they are offering. Karan now wants to look at all the buyers, but he would prefer the buyers offering a higher price over others. Help Karan sort all the buyers in descending order of the price offered.

<b>INPUT</b>:
First line contains the n denoting the number of brokers.
Next n lines contain a series of integers(sorted in descending order) each denoting the list of buyers given by each broker. Each line end with -1.

<b>OUTPUT</b>:
A single line containing the prices offered by all buyers in descending order.

<b>EXAMPLE</b>:
INPUT: 
2
6 3 -1
4 2 -1
OUTPUT: 6 4 3 2
Explanation:
All the buyers are arranged in the descending order of the price they offered.
