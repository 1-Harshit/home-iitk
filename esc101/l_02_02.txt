                <div style="white-space: pre-wrap" class="statement"><h1 id="buying-chocolate">Buying Chocolate</h1>
<p>Ayush has received <code>M</code> rupees from his father today. He goes to a nearby chocolate store. The shopkeeper shows Ayush <code>N</code> options, each with different prices. Ayush loves chocolates, so he wants to buy the most expensive one, which falls under his budget. Ayush is weak in numbers and needs your help.</p>
<h2 id="input">Input</h2>
<p>The first line contains two natural numbers <code>N</code> and <code>M</code>. The second line has <code>N</code> distinct natural numbers separated by spaces indicating the price of <code>N</code> chocolates.</p>
<h2 id="output">Output</h2>
<p>Print the price of the chocolate which Ayush should buy.
Note: If Ayush cannot afford any chocolate, then return -1.</p>
