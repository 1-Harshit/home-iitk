                <div style="white-space: pre-wrap" class="statement">Anany loves playing with geometrical objects. He needs your help in finding some properties of a given cylinder. You are given <i>radius(r)</i>, <i>height(h)</i> of a solid cylinder which are positive numbers. You need to calculate the following:
  1. Volume of the cylinder
  2. Curved surface area of the cylinder.
  3. Ratio of first two quantities.

<b>Input:</b> 
  Input contains 2 floating point numbers <i>r</i> and <i>h</i> (0 &lt;= <i>r,h</i> &lt;= 500).

<b>Output:</b> 
  Output the above mentioned measures in the following format(upto 3 decimal points) - 
  Curved Surface Area : <i>your answer</i>
  Volume : <i>your answer</i>
  Ratio of curved surface area to volume : <i>your answer</i>

<b>Note</b> - Assume value of <i>pi</i> to be 3.14159
See sample output for the required format.

<b>Example:</b>
<b>Input:</b>
3 1.5
<b>Output:</b>
Curved Surface Area : 28.274
Volume : 42.411
Ratio of curved surface area to volume : 0.667
