                <h1><span class="title">Game of primes</span><br><small class="category">Bonus5</small></h1>
                <hr>
                <div style="white-space: pre-wrap" class="statement">In the Primeland, there lives a monk who has a special power to check the integrity of a couple. The integrity of a couple can be expressed as a function of the primality of their love attribute. 

Couple (x,y) has the integrity as follows ( x and y are their respective attributes): 
If x and y are both prime, then it is x+y
If only x is prime, then it is x
If only y is prime, it is y
Else, it is x*y

<b>Guideline :</b>
Make sure that you code using FUNCTIONS to obtain maximum marks. YOU WILL LOSE MARKS IF THIS GUIDELINE IS NOT FOLLOWED

<b>Input Format:</b>
Two space separated positive integers, x and y.

<b>Output Format:</b>
A single line consisting of the integrity of couple.
