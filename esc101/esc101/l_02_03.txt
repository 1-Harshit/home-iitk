                <div style="white-space: pre-wrap" class="statement"><h1 id="voldemort-s-favorite-number">Voldemort's Favorite Number</h1>
<p>It is Lord Voldemort's birthday. His faithful set of servants gift him a list of numbers.
Lord Voldemort wants to find the count of numbers which have sum of digits greater than or equal to 7 (since 7 is his favourite number).</p>
<p>He is weak in programming and thus wants your help to do it.</p>
<h2 id="input">Input</h2>
<p>You will be given one line of input containing of list of Integers. You need to stop reading whenever you encounter a <b>-1</b>.</p>
<h2 id="output">Output</h2>
<p>Output the count of numbers which have sum of digits greater that equal to 7.</p>
<h2 id="examples">Examples</h2>
<h3 id="example-1">Example 1</h3>
<h4 id="input-">Input:</h4>
<pre><code><span class="hljs-number">-8</span> <span class="hljs-number">2</span> <span class="hljs-number">589</span> <span class="hljs-number">-4</span> <span class="hljs-number">5</span> <span class="hljs-number">-1</span>
</code></pre><h4 id="output-">Output:</h4>
<pre><code><span class="hljs-number">2</span>
</code></pre><h4 id="explanation-">Explanation:</h4>
<p>-8 and 589 have sum of digits 8 and 22 (satisfying &gt;=7). 2, -4 and 5 have sum of digits as 2, 4 and 5 (not satisfying). </p>
<h3 id="note-">Note:</h3>
<p>For negative numbers, sum of digits is taken simply without considering the negative sign.</p>
