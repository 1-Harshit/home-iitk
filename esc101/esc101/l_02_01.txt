                <div style="white-space: pre-wrap" class="statement"><h1 id="skipping-stones">Skipping Stones</h1>
<p>You have a number line with a stone placed at each integer position. You are standing at position <code>a</code>, and your destination is <code>b</code>.</p>
<p>Unfortunately, you can take jumps of exactly <code>n</code> positions at a time. Hence, it may not always be possible to reach <code>b</code>. So, you have to get as close to <code>b</code> as possible, such that the final position is less than or at <code>b</code>.</p>
<h2 id="input">Input</h2>
<p>You will be given three numbers in the same line separated by a space in the following format:</p>
<p><code>a b n</code></p>
<p>Note: <code>n</code> will be a positive integer.</p>
<h2 id="output">Output</h2>
<p>The output will consist of two lines.</p>
<p>In the first line, print your position after each step starting from <code>a</code> to your final position.</p>
<p>In the second line, print the number of steps required.</p>
