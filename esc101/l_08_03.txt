                <div style="white-space: pre-wrap" class="statement"><h1>Roll me One</h1>
You will be given two integers m and n and an array of mn elements.
You have to roll the array into a matrix of m rows and n columns.
The first element of the array should occupy (0,0) position in the matrix, and then the rest of the elements are to be filled in clockwise in a spiral fashion.
Output the rolled matrix.

<b>Sample testcase</b>
Input:
5 6
3 1 2 3 4 6 8 0 7 2 6 1 9 2 1 3 5 3 4 8 7 5 5 6 2 4 1 3 6 8
Output:
3 1 2 3 4 6
3 4 8 7 5 8
5 3 6 8 5 0
3 1 4 2 6 7
1 2 9 1 6 2</div>
