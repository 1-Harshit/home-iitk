                <div style="white-space: pre-wrap" class="statement"><h1 id="intersection-point">Merge the arrays</h1>
<p>You are given two sorted arrays. Merge the two arrays to create another sorted array such that there are no repeated elements in it.</p>
<h2 id="input">Input</h2>
<p>The first line of input will contain 2 integers, <code>m</code> and <code>n</code>. <code>m</code> is the size of first array, and <code>n</code> is the size of second array.</p>
<p>The next two lines contain <code>m</code> and <code>n</code> integers respectively.</p>

<h2 id="output">Output</h2>
<p>The output will consist of two lines.</p>
<p>First line should contain the length of merged array.</p>
<p>Second line contains the elements of merged array.</p>
<h2 id="examples">Example</h2>
<h4 id="input-">Input:</h4>
<pre><code><span class="hljs-symbol">4 5<br>1 3 3 6<br>2 4 5 6 8</span>
</code></pre>
<h4 id="output-">Output:</h4>
<pre><code>7<br>1 2 3 4 5 6 8
</code></pre>
<b>Note: You are not allowed to use the [ ] operator. Also, do not change the function signature of the "merge" function. Doing either of these will fetch 0 marks.</b>
