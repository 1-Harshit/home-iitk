                <div style="white-space: pre-wrap" class="statement">You will receive as input a (char) capital alphabet and an (int) number (non-negative, less than 26). Write a C program that takes this input and prints as output the character shifted forward by that number.
If in the process of shifting forward, you jump forward from 'Z', you should start again from 'A'.

Sample Testcases

Testcase 1
Input:
D
4
Output:
H
Explanation:
4 jumps from D: D -&gt; E -&gt; F -&gt; G -&gt; H

Testcase 2
Input:
Y
3
Output:
B
Explanation:
3 jumps from Y, and restarting from A, after Z: Y -&gt; Z -&gt; A -&gt; B</div>
